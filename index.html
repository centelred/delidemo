About The Deli Basket


A Local Charm Since 2011

Here at The Deli Basket we serve freshly sliced meats and cheeses to create the ultimate deli experience. All of our sandwiches and salads are made to order, ensuring your satisfaction. Our customers are really important to us, and our friendly staff makes certain you'll love every last bite. We guarantee it!

We offer dine-in, carryout, and catering. We also deliver up to a 5 mile radius! Call in or stop by today!
